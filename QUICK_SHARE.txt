To make app public (NO PASSWORD):

1. Open new terminal
2. Run this command:
   npx localtunnel --port 3000

3. Copy the URL shown (like: https://abc.loca.lt)
4. Share that URL with testers!

That's it! NO PASSWORD NEEDED!

OR for SSH tunnel (also no password):
   ssh -R 80:localhost:3000 serveo.net

Copy-paste this command - it's free!
